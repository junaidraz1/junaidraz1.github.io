Meet People & Integrate 2.0

On the day before Valentine’s Day, Finland’s Friendship Day (ystävänpäivä), Mercatori at the Turku School of Economics hosted the Meet People & Integrate 2.0 event, bringing together international degree students. The programme included an international dessert potluck, cultural stalls, a best-dressed competition, a multicultural quiz, and an icebreaker bingo, offering participants an opportunity to meet new people and share their experiences of settling in Turku.
